Gaelic Football

My favourite sport is Gaelic Football. I play with D***** Gaa Club on the under 21s team. I have been playing football since I was 13 and I go training twice a week. Every year I go to the All Ireland Final in Croke Park. Cork have won the All Ireland every year from 2011-2016 but unfortunately their winning streak was ruined by Dublin in 2017 who also took the title in 2018.
